Written Description of Observable trends:

Close to 85% of players are male and between the ages of 15-34. Players between the ages of 20-24 represent the biggest
group with close to 45% of total players. In addition, players between 15-34 have a higher percentage of buying more than
one game (They have as a group a higher percentage of overall games bought per people within their groups).Lastly, there 
seems to be a direct correlation between how popular a game is and how expensive it is, and total sales generated. Meaning 
that the price of a game is 'tied up' to its demand.
